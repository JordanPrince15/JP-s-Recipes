Espresso Chocolate Cake

A decadent dessert for coffee lovers!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup brewed espresso
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Combine dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Add espresso, buttermilk, and oil, and mix until smooth.
  4. Pour into a greased pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  5. Let cool before frosting.
