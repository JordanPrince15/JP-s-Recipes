Vanilla Cake

A lovely recipe for any cake lover!

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup coconut flour
  • 1½ cups almond flour
  • 4 eggs
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon powder
  • ⅛ tsp baking soda
  • ⅓ cup ghee
  • ¼ to ⅓ cup honey
  • 2 TBSP Basic Coconut Milk
  • ...

Vanilla Frosting ingredients

  • 1½ cup raw whole cashew nuts
  • ⅓ cup honey
  • ½ cup Basic Coconut Milk (see Related Recipes)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

    • Instructions

    1. Pre-heat the oven at 350°F (180°C or gas mark 4)
    2. For the cake, combine all the ingredients together in a food processor until smooth.
    3. Pour the batter into a 9-by-12-inch sheet pan that has been greased (with ghee) and allow it to bake for approximately 30-45 minutes.
    4. Make the frosting while the cake is baking,
    5. When the cake has cooled down, spread the frosting generously on top if you want a one layer cake,
    6. Or alternately, you could cut the sheet cake in half and make a two-layer cake. In this case, you would add the frosting between the layers first, and then around and on top of the cake.
